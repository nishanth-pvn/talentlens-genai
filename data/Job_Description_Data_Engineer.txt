POSITION: Senior Data Engineer
LOCATION: Singapore
DEPARTMENT: IT - Data & Analytics
EMPLOYMENT TYPE: Full-time

JOB SUMMARY:
We are seeking an experienced Data Engineer to design, build, and maintain scalable data pipelines and infrastructure supporting our pharmaceutical research and clinical trial operations. This role is critical in ensuring our data infrastructure supports business intelligence, analytics, and machine learning initiatives.

REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS:
• 5+ years of experience in data engineering roles
• Strong programming skills in Python and SQL
• Experience with cloud platforms (AWS/Azure/GCP) and cloud-native data services
• Expertise in building ETL/ELT pipelines using modern tools
• Proficiency with big data technologies (Apache Spark, Kafka, Airflow)
• Experience with data warehousing solutions (Snowflake, Redshift, BigQuery)
• Strong understanding of data modeling, database design, and optimization
• Experience with version control (Git) and CI/CD practices
• Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field

PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS:
• Experience in pharmaceutical, healthcare, or life sciences industry
• Knowledge of data governance frameworks and regulatory compliance (GDPR, HIPAA, GxP)
• Experience with real-time data processing and streaming architectures
• Familiarity with containerization (Docker, Kubernetes)
• Experience building data pipelines for machine learning applications
• AWS/Azure/GCP professional certifications
• Master's degree in related field

KEY RESPONSIBILITIES:
• Design, develop, and maintain scalable data pipelines and ETL processes
• Build and optimize data warehouse solutions for analytics and reporting
• Implement data quality frameworks and monitoring systems
• Optimize data infrastructure for performance, reliability, and cost-efficiency
• Collaborate with data scientists, analysts, and business stakeholders
• Ensure compliance with data governance and security standards
• Create technical documentation and data dictionaries
• Mentor junior engineers and promote best practices
• Evaluate and implement new data technologies and tools
